About Us

The Campus to Corporate (C2C) Club at Chandigarh University is designed to equip students with essential skills for a smooth transition from academic life to the professional world. Through workshops, seminars, and mentorship programs, we focus on bridging the gap between classroom learning and industry expectations.

Our mission is to foster holistic development by enhancing soft skills, aptitude, communication, and professional etiquette, ensuring that every student is industry-ready and confident to face corporate challenges.

What We Focus On

Corporate Skill Development

Training on tools, technologies, and workflows used in top-tier companies.

Communication & Soft Skills

Mastering public speaking, presentation, and interpersonal communication.

Industry Readiness

Mock interviews, group discussions, and etiquette training.

Resume & LinkedIn Building

Crafting impactful resumes and optimizing professional social media profiles.

Aptitude & Interview Prep

Problem-solving sessions and logic building for placement exams.

Professional Networking

Connecting with alumni and industry experts for guidance and opportunities.
